Failing with friends

You learn from your failures. Let’s say you fail 20 times in a year.
If you have five friends that are also working on the same type of things. They may fail around 20 times too.
If you’re sharing those failures with each other. You failed 100 times in that year.
By sharing each others failures, you lived 5 years of failures in one year. Exponentially increasing your chances to succeed.
